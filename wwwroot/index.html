WELCOME @ DUET

DUET is a patterndesign tool.

You can create beautiful proffesional patterns with DUET.
We will inspire you with some of the beautiful screenprinted designs of Jolanta Izabela Pawlak.

Take a look at these beautiful scarfs. These are unique screen-prints on fabric. If you can enjoy the beautiful floral- and coral-textures, then we invite you to explore this site a little bit more. Jolanta was inspired by the beautiful textures of the corals in the ocean nearby Curacao. We hope you will get inspired by these scarfs and design your own patterns with a digital stamp technique in this tool, created by Ria Stroes, creative coder.

In this app you will explore these beautiful textures and use them for your own designs.

DUET Membership

We ask you to register, so we can save your designs. We need to identify you when you visit our site the next time.

We will save your designs in a big-format, so they can be used for printing. Your designs will be created on the server in a maximum size of 48 x 48 cm. Every design you create is a pattern that can be repeated horizontal and vertical.

If you want to order your designs on paper or fabric, you have to fill in all the fields in the form.

You will be the owner of these designs and you can check, show, share and order these designs.

You can unsubscribe any time and you can delete all or some of your designs anytime.

We hope you will have fun with our designtool DUET.
