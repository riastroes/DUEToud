Inspiration

Get inspired bij these beautiful textures. In this tool you can use them by clicking on a area of the print.
So you can select your backgroundcolor for your design and ﬁll your stamp with textures and colors.
Click on the lock to stop the carousel-function of the inspiration area. Click the lock again to restart the carousel-function.

Settings

On this panel you can ﬁnd all the functions you can use to create your own design The panel can move to the left- and rightside of your screen.
Opening the inspiration area or the designer area.

  • Button Designer/Inspiration click open the designer area.
  • Button Inspiration/Designer click open the inspiration area.

Tip! You can also click anywhere on the panel and it will move to the other side.

Background

Here is your pallet for your background colors. Fill this pallet bij clicking on the inspiration prints. you will start with a white background.
Click on the pallet to set the backgroundcolor of your design.
Click om the basket to empty your pallet.

My Stamp

On the left side you see your stamp. This is the tool you will use to create, stamping your own design on the canvas in the designer area.
You can change this stamp in many ways. On the right side you can select the shape of your stamp.
There are 6 different stamp shapes. If you click on the inspiration prints you can ﬁll your stamp with colors and textures.
There are 3 different stamp types:

  • copy stamp, 1 to 1 copy of a small area of your inspiration print.
  • color stamp, only a copy of one color you click on in the inspiration print.
  • lightness stamp, all the colors with the same lightness in a area you click on in the inspiration print. This can be light or dark colors.

Stamp Behavour

Repeat horizontal 1 to 10 times

If you stamp on the canvas your stamp will be repeated 1 to 10 times over the full with of your pattern or in a part of your canvas depending of the repeat distance.

Repeat vertical 1 to 10 times

If you stamp on the canvas your stamp will be repeated 1 to 10 times over the full with of your pattern or in a part of your canvas depending of the repeat distance.

Repeat distance

If repeat distance is set to 100 % the repeat stamp functions will affect the full width or height of the canvas. If it you set to 50%, it will affect half of your canvas

Scale

Scale the stamp, smaller or bigger. If you scale up you will lose some sharpness in your design.

Rotate

Rotate your stamp.

Use your stamp as a brush

A difficult but fun option to play with. Hold your right mousebutton down when you move your stamp over the canvas.

Buttons

  • Designer / Inspiration button, move the settingspanel to the left or right.
  • New, start wih a new design.
  • Delete, clear your canvas and start over.
  • Save Design, save your design and wait until it is saved.
